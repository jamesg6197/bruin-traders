Bruin Traders

Bruin Traders is a group of students from diverse technical backgrounds who all share a strong
interest in the field of trading and quantitative finance.

With the growing interest in trading and quantitative finance, we hope to provide students
with learning and networking opportunities through info sessions and a workshop series, with more
possible events in the future.

Education

Technical workshops for UCLA students are hosted to teach skills and knowledge used in trading and research

Networking

We host info sessions and networking opportunities with our sponsors to connect students with industry professionals

Community

We develop a community by not only supporting students academically but also creating life-long connections and friendships
